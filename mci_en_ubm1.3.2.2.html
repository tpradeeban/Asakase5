Setting range for Cycle

The usable cycle ranges in the universal buffer memory are as follows.

Setting range for Cycle.
Tester nameRange
T5833UT_SIG_FL,UT_SIG_RD,UT_SIG_WT,UT_SIG_C(0-31),UT_SIG_M(1-2)
T5831UT_SIG_FL,UT_SIG_RD,UT_SIG_WT,UT_SIG_C(0-31),UT_SIG_M(1-2)
T5830UT_SIG_FL,UT_SIG_RD,UT_SIG_WT,UT_SIG_C(0-31),UT_SIG_M(1-2)
T5822UT_SIG_FL,UT_SIG_RD,UT_SIG_WT,UT_SIG_C(0-31),UT_SIG_M(1-2)
T5781UT_SIG_FL,UT_SIG_RD,UT_SIG_WT,UT_SIG_C(0-31),UT_SIG_M(1-2)
T5761UT_SIG_FL,UT_SIG_C(0-31)
T5588UT_SIG_FL,UT_SIG_RD,UT_SIG_WT,UT_SIG_C(0-23),UT_SIG_M(1-2)
T5503HS2UT_SIG_FL,UT_SIG_RD,UT_SIG_WT,UT_SIG_C(0-31),UT_SIG_M(1-2),UT_SIG_E(0-3)
T5503HSUT_SIG_FL,UT_SIG_RD,UT_SIG_WT,UT_SIG_C(0-31),UT_SIG_M(1-2)
T5385UT_SIG_FL,UT_SIG_RD,UT_SIG_WT,UT_SIG_C(0-31),UT_SIG_M(1-2)
T5383UT_SIG_FL,UT_SIG_RD,UT_SIG_WT,UT_SIG_C(0-31),UT_SIG_M(1-2)
W5320UT_SIG_FL,UT_SIG_C(0-11)

Because UT_SIG_FL is the initial value, UBM does not operate even if it is specified.
