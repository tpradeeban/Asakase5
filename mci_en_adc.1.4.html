Additional Notes

  1. UTL_SendAdc() and UTL_MeasDct()

    UTL_MeasDct() overwrites the hardware conditions set by UTL_SendAdc().

    To perform DC parametric tests in pattern program after executing UTL_MeasDct() or UTL_SendAdc() need to be used to reset the hardware conditions.
