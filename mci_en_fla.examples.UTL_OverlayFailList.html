Example of using UTL_OverlayFailList()

Below is an example to read failure information from the Fail DB, with two test names specified that are going to be overlaid. 



    UTL_SetFailListAccessTestSelect   (flAH, UT_FL_TESTSEL_NAME);
    UTL_ClearFailListAccessTestName   (flAH);
    UTL_AddFailListAccessTestName     (flAH, "tname1");
    UTL_AddFailListAccessTestName     (flAH, "tname2");
    UTL_SetFailListAccessDeviceChannel(flAH, 1);
    UTL_SetFailListAccessAllDut       (flAH, UT_ON);

    UTL_OverlayFailList               (flAH, UT_OFF);

    UTL_SetFailListAccessTestSelect   (flAH, UT_FL_TESTSEL_OVERLAY);

If the argument is UT_OFF as shown, Fail DB of tname1 and tname2 are kept.

If the argument is UT_ON, Fail DB of tname1 and tname2 are deleted.

The last line means the overlay results of tname1 and tname2 will be retrieved from the Fail DB.
