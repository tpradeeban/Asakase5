CAUTION

The source sync comparator can be used for the following systems:

  • T5833

  • T5832

The source sync comparator converts differential input of Posi and Nega pins into single-ended output and performs comparison judgments.

Comparison judgment is made based on VOH and VOL on the Posi pin, and the result is also stored on the Posi pin side.

  • In terms of hardware, Posi and Nega pins are paired for operation as follows:
