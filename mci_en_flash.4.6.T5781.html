T5781

  • The number of site chains is 8 or less.

  • The number of DUTs tested in parallel within a site is 4 or less.

  • Pins (for which "P" is specified), which are assigned to one DUT, are not assigned to multiple sites.

    If "PD" is specified for those pins, they can be assigned to multiple sites.

    When a site chain is specified, pin assignment for each site is as follows:
